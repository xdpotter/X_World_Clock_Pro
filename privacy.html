Privacy Policy

At X World Clock, your privacy is important to us. We designed this website to be simple, safe, and easy to use. We do not collect personal information such as your name, phone number, or precise location. This page explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

We only collect basic, non-personal information to help us improve the site:

  • Your browser type (for example: Chrome, Safari)
  • Your device type (mobile or desktop)
  • Anonymous usage data (pages viewed, general time on site)

This information is aggregated and used only to make the website faster and easier to use.

Cookies

We use small files called cookies to:

  • Keep the site working smoothly
  • Remember simple preferences
  • Help show relevant ads

You can turn off cookies anytime in your browser settings. Turning off some cookies may affect how the site works.
